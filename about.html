Our Story

Mississippi native Ryan Connor founded Blade Slinger in 2021 after nearly a decade refining his skills in landscaping as a Landscape Foreman. His passion evolved into a successful business built on quality and pride.

As Blade Slinger expanded, customers connected deeply with the brand — inspiring the launch of Blade Slinger merchandise, including hats, shirts, decals, and accessories.

Today, Blade Slinger continues to grow by offering professional lawn and landscaping services alongside its merchandise line.

Our Mission

We promise to provide high‑quality lawn care, landscaping services, and merchandise at fair market prices. Our value is in the detail — we treat your lawn like our own.

Whether improving curb appeal or shipping out a new Blade Slinger performance hat, our mission remains the same: deliver honest value and build a brand Mississippi homeowners are proud of.
