Our Story

How We Started

Mississippi native Ryan Connor founded Blade Slinger in 2021 after nearly a decade refining his skills and expertise in landscaping as a Landscape Foreman. Since graduating college and working in the industry, Connor’s passion for landscaping has evolved into a successful business. What began as a one‑man operation has grown into a trusted service provider known for reliability, craftsmanship, and a commitment to helping homeowners maintain clean, healthy, and well‑designed outdoor spaces. As Blade Slinger expanded, Connor recognized that customers connected deeply with the brand he was building — not just the services. That demand inspired the launch of Blade Slinger merchandise, a curated line of hats, shirts, decals, and accessories designed for clients who take pride in their lawns, their community, and the Blade Slinger name. What started as a small batch of logo decals quickly evolved into a full online storefront, allowing customers across Mississippi to represent the brand with high‑quality apparel and gear. Today, Blade Slinger continues to grow by offering a full range of professional lawn and landscaping services alongside its popular merchandise line. Connor’s focus on quality, transparency, and long‑term customer relationships has earned the company a reputation for excellence. Whether improving a property’s curb appeal or shipping out the latest Blade Slinger performance hat, the mission remains the same: deliver value, take pride in the work, and build a brand that Mississippi homeowners are proud to stand behind.
